What exactly is Statistexts? Is that even a real word?

Statistexts is an Android application that allows you to see exactly how responsive your friends are. The app gathers statistics about your text messages, hence Statistexts. The app analyzes all of the text messages on your phone and allows you to see how your friends rank in comparison to each other over many different statistics including Fastest to Reply, Most Positive, Slowest to Reply, and many more. Below are features that Statistexts has.

Main Page

 The main page is displayed upon startup of the app. From the main page you can choose 'Load Texts' or go to the Settings menu. When you load texts, the application will display a progress bar indicating how many text messages are left to be analyzed. This information is stored in the app so you don't have to analyze all messages every time you run the app. Instead, only messages that haven't been analyzed yet are used.

Sort Your Friends

Upon choosing 'Load Texts' on the main screen, Statistexts will analyze your messages and then take you to this page, which ranks your Contacts on the chosen sort method. The default is to sort based on the fastest response time. In other words, these are the people that reply to you the fastest.

Contact Statistics

When you click on a contact on the Contact List page, you will be taken to a page that displays all of the statistics about the selected contact. Clicking on the orange field titles will give you more information about the field while clicking on the actual data will display a graph about the information. Finally, you can search for individual words to see how often you and the contact said something.

Informative Graphs

Clicking on a data point on the individual contact page will show you a graph pertinent to the selected data. Shown above is a graph that relates how the contact's response time has changed over time. These graphs are generated by Google Graphs and therefore you need internet to see them.

Sorting

Back on the Contact List page, there is a drop-down menu that allows you to choose how you want to sort yourr contact list. The default is to sort by Fastest Average Response Time but you can choose many others. The options include sorting by "best" and "worst" of most statistics stored. This means you can sort by most verbose friend or least verbose friend, for instance.

You

At the bottom of the Contacts List page, you will notice a button that says 'Statistics About You'. Clicking this button will bring you to an individual contact page much like the one for regular contacts. Just like a regular contact, clicking on the field titles will display information about the specified field and clicking on the field values will pop up a graph about the statistic.
