This app is compatible with all of your devices.

Statistexts is an application that allows users to analyze their text messages and see interesting details about each contact such as the average time it takes the contact to reply to messages, when they are the most responsive, and how positive they are in the messages. Clicking on any of the details will display a graph that gives more detailed information. For example, clicking on a contact's average response time will show you a graph that displays average response time over time so you will know how that contact has changed in relation to their contact with you over time!

You can sort contacts by any of the individual metrics so you can see who is most quick to respond among other things. Sorting can also be done in reverse. For instance, you can see who is the least positive in messages. Further, you can sort by your qualities to see who you are fastest to reply to, etc.

You can also search for specific words between contacts to see how often you or the other person has said something and how much of the total messaging volume that the searched term contributed to.

For more info, go to the app's official website: www.statistexts.com
