MERMAID ME

I want to be a merman.
This is basically my application

  • Qualifications

Why?

Everyone has a calling. Some people do important things, such as save lives. I may not be as good at saving lives as my beautiful girlfriend Emily, but does that make my calling any less important? Probably. However, a calling is a calling and I believe I need to fulfill my destiny of being a merperson at an aquarium or amusement park.

Qualifications

The ability to swim long distances, sometimes in the tens of meters.

The ability to wield a trident, the official mermaid weapon of choice. Although I am not combat capable, I can definitely lift one, which I believe is a plus.

The ability to open my eyes under water. This is vital because mermaids look really stupid wearing goggles.

Finally, I possess the ability to hold my breath for prolonged periods of time, upwards of over a minute. It is with deep regret that I admit that I do not have gills.
