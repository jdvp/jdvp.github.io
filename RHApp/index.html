RHApp

Just an incredibly life changing health app for Rice students by Rice students.

Have you ever been really sick and didn't want to walk all the way to your local RHA's room only to find out that they weren't there? Have you ever really needed condoms RIGHT NOW and there were no available RHA's ANYWHERE? Have you ever been a Hanszenite hanging out at Martel and you felt like you needed a little medication in your life but you didn't know any of the RHAs at Martel so you had to either choose to walk all the way back to your college or suffer the pains of the world?

Well this is the app for you.

Things I Can Do With RHApp

There are many things you can do with RHApp

  • Acquire Condoms
  • locate available RHAs
  • Send questions to RHAs
  • RHAs can log in to answer questions
  • Find out which RHAs have which supplies
  • Become closer friends with an RHA

A Quick Peek

You'll be blown away at how beautiful our app is. Not only is it super functional, but it is something that you wouldn't mind looking at for 20 hours a day. Trust us.

Main Page

The main page is where you login. You have to be a Rice student in order to use the app. This is for the safety and privacy of Rice students. Notice that in keeping with our health theme, the owl's eyes are condoms.

Tell RHAs What You NEED

Let an RHA know what you need and someone will be sure to get back to you! RHAs can answer these messages and requests and let you know that they can fill your request!

Sort the RHAs

You can choose to view RHAs from only a certain college! In the future, it may be possible to give RHAs ratings and sort by friendliest or most attractive!
