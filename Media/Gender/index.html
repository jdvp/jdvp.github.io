Generally, most of us believe we conform to a certain set of gender roles based on DNA or pre-disposed knowledge. Gender Roles, which are created and policed by society, are sprung upon us unconsciously from the day we are born. At birth, the sex of a new born usually determines the tone, and also the color of clothing a baby will first be wrapped in. From this day forth society and the mass media subconsciously illustrates a normative ideology of what is necessary to identify or behave as a certain gender.
