Adolescents and Media

Exploring the effects that various types of Media have on adolescents.

Overview

As an adolescent develops, they come into contact with many external stimuli that affect the ways that they think and act. These outside influences include friends, family, teachers, religious leaders. However, these influences don't have to be people and can also include various forms of media and social media as it becomes increasingly prolific and easier to access. We sought out the effects that media has on adolescents' psycho-social development and had many questions that we wanted to answer.

How has an increased use of social media affected adolescent development? Does playing video games make adolescents more aggressive? How do gender represenations in media affect the behavior of adolescents? If there are negative effects of any of the various types of media, are there any organizations trying to combat these negative effects?
