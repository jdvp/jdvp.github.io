As an adolescent develops, they come into contact with many external stimuli that affect the ways that they think and act. These outside influences include friends, family, teachers, religious leaders. However, these influences don't have to be people and can also include various forms of media and social media as it becomes increasingly prolific and easier to access. We sought out the effects that media has on adolescents and had many questions that we wanted to answer.

What are the effects of media on adolescents? Is the increasing use of social media detrimental to adolescents' in-person social skills?
