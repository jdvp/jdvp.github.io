Stage 5: Ego Identity vs. Role Confusion

 

During this psychological crisis, adolescents can be at:

 

Moratorium (have not committed to their identities)

Achievement (made commitments to a fixed identity)

 

•Currently, the struggles of being in moratorium are played out online, rather than face to face.

 

•Social media is a convenient outlet for young people to shuffle through parts of their representation in the process of settling their identity

 

•These adolescents are more at risk for disclosing more intimate information than the adolescents who have committed to their identities
