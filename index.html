Professional Experience

I'm currently employed as a professional Android Developer. Although I'm a Texas native, I've been living out of state since I graduated from college. I lived in Columbus, Ohio for 5 years and really liked it but moved to Seattle this past year to try something new.

After graduating from Rice University, I was employed at JP Morgan working on their Chase Pay mobile application. I helped shape its initial public release in November 2016 and continued work on it until its 2.0 release.

At that point, I moved over to L Brands and focused my work on the PINK Nation and Victoria's Secret Android apps, although I had to work on the Bath & Body Works app on occasion. My primary focus at L Brands was leading Android development on an internal Shopping library that powered the shopping experience in both the VS and PINK applications.

As of May 2022 I am working at Zillow as a Senior SDE focused on the Android app.

For more detailed information on my professional experiences, take a look at my profile on LinkedIn!

Active Personal Projects

These are projects that I am actively engaged on and improving

jekyll-comments-google-forms

jekyll-comments-google-forms is a tool that enables free commenting functionality on Jekyll-generated static sites by leveraging Google Sheets as a backend.

If interested, take a look at the source code on the Github Project page or check the associated article for a more in-depth explanation.

Inactive Projects

These are projects that I am no longer working on for various reasons. If you happen to use any of them though and have an idea for an update, feel free to reach out and let me know!

Notification Launcher

Notification Launcher is an Android app that allows scheduling of notifications for any app on your phone. It also can be used to share reminders from other apps.

See the old release notes page for more details.

Statistexts

Statistexts is an Android application that allows users to see how responsive their contacts are and how they compare to each other.

See more details on its official website.

Animosaic

Animosaic is a web app that lets users create mosaics of images using the cover art from multiple anime series. The duck mosaic above was made using the app.

You can check it out here!

TextMessageViewer

TextMessageViewer is a Java executable that allows users to view SMS backup files generated by apps such as SMS Backup & Restore on their PC.

More info and download links can be found here.
