About Me

I am a recent computer science graduate from Rice University in Houston, Texas. I am currently employed as a TAP Analyst at JPMC. I hope to learn many new things and make invaluable connections here.
My interests include Mobile Development (check out my app Statistexts) and Web Development, which I took a class on last semester. Currently I am working on a web application that helps people track their workouts and view progress over time. I really like to learn new things and in the future I would like to learn more about the MEAN stack and potentially do some video game creation with LibGDX.

My favorite programming language is Java because I have been using it the longest (since freshman year of highschool), but I can see the appeal of basically any language. The language you use is just a tool to complete a project, so why not use the best tool for the job?

In my free time I like to go to the gym or work on various projects (usually things that I think would be cool or helpful to me personally but do not exist). Other than that, I find myself playing video games, such as DotA 2, pretty often. I'm pretty bad at DotA but I have a good time playing with my friends so I'm not too worried about it!
