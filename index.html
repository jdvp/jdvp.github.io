About Me

I am a senior computer science student currently studying at Rice University in Houston, Texas. I will be graduating in May, so I am also currently searching for a job (which might as well be a job in and of itself!). My current interests are Mobile Development (check out my app Statistexts) and WebDev, which I am taking a class on. I really like to learn new things and in the future I would like to learn more about the MEAN stack and potentially do some video game creation with LibGDX.

My favorite programming language is Java, and (maybe oddly) I like it because of all of the structure it has. Whereas some people say that it is too verbose or has too much boilerplate code, I find it kind of nice in a way.

Sometimes, when I'm not trying to decide on what my favorite programming languages are or doing homework I like to go to the gym and work on various random projects or play video games (like DotA 2, which I am unfortunately terrible at playing).
